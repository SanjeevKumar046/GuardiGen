GaurdiGen makes forgetting your password a thing of the past. Each password is uniquely crafted based on your account details. Forget it? No worries! Just regenerate it using the same secret key. Just remember, keep that secret key… well, a secret!

The Key is the Key!

Your secret key is as unique as your fingerprint. Choose one that even Sherlock Holmes couldn't crack. Throw in some symbols, uppercase letters, and numbers for that extra oomph against hackers.

GuardiGen plays referee—it'll let you know if your secret key is a touchdown or needs a pep talk. Remember, keep your secret key under wraps.
